FAQ

Questions, answered.

Everything you need to know before you sign up.

Do I need to know how to code to attend? +
Not at all. This workshop is designed for designers — not engineers. Day 2 covers tools and building, but everything is accessible without a coding background. Pavan built Justlog without being a professional developer.
I'm a junior designer with less than 2 years experience. Is this too advanced? +
This workshop was built for all levels. Junior designers actually have an advantage — you're learning AI-native thinking before any bad habits form. Day 1 and Day 3 are especially valuable for early-career positioning.
Will sessions be recorded if I miss one? +
Yes. All 3 sessions will be recorded and shared with registered participants. You'll have permanent access to the recordings.
What tools do I need to participate? +
Just a laptop and a stable internet connection. Figma (free tier is fine) is helpful but not required. All other tools discussed will be introduced during the workshop.
What language will the workshop be conducted in? +
The workshop will be conducted in English with context given in Hindi where helpful. All materials are in English.
